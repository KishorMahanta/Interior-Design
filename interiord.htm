About Us

We Create Functional Spaces With Soul

Founded in 2010, Flux Interior Design Studio has been at the forefront of innovative and sustainable interior design. We believe that well-designed spaces can transform lives and inspire creativity.

Our team of passionate designers combines artistic vision with practical solutions to create spaces that are not only beautiful but also functional and reflective of our clients' unique personalities and lifestyles.
